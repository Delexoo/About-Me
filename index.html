Free University Tools

This website started as a simple idea: a collection of useful tools for everyone. At first, it was called UTIL-Framework. The early versions weren't great, the design was messy, the flow felt off, and it wasn't something I was proud to share.

When I started college, I realized how many resources students actually need, books, collaboration tools, AI tools, and more. That's when UTIL-Framework evolved into quoteai.org, a more advanced version of the idea. But it still wasn't enough.

Over time, I improved the design, clarified the purpose, made everything more professional and enhanced marketing. That's how quoteai.org became freeuniversitytools.com.

Now, Free University Tools is a project I'm proud of, built to give students easy access to the tools they need, all in one place.

Visit: freeuniversitytools.com
